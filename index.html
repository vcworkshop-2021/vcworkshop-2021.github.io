Overview

We are excited to announce the MIT Visual Computing Workshop!

We invite all the MIT VC faculties and students to join this workshop. This year, we are glad to have Professor Alexei Efros and Professor Jessica Hodgins join our workshop. They will give a presentation about their failure on research and have a discussion with participants after the talk.
